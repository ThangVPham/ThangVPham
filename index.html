About Me

Hello there,

My name is Thang and I am a software developer based in Toronto, Canada. I have 3+ years of professional experience working in a highly technical field prior to transitioning into software engineering. The exposure to the industrial automation software development in my previous career led me to discover a new interest in programming, which gradually became a passion.

I enjoy working in team environment, and have taken on the leadership role for multiple successful team-based projects during my time in school. For this effort, I was honored to have been selected as a recipient of one of the Leadership Scholarships at Centennial College, awarded to student who has demonstrated leadership in their community or program.

Currently my plan is to be able to contribute to the accessibility of the Web. My belief is that as the Information Revolution rapidly unfolds, and the Web becomes an essential part of our daily lives, we need to make an effort to be more inclusive by providing an easier way for people with certain disabilities to access the online contents with ease.

Please feel free to reach out if you would like to know more about me. I am always open for new opportunities for collaboration.
